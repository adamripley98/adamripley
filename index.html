Founder
This was a charity project I started back in high school aimed at incentivizing cleaning up trash. We would find especially dirty places in Anchorage and hide cash among some of the trash. By the end of the project, we had 1500+ facebook followers, were featured in the news, and had made Anchorage 200+ bags of trash cleaner.
